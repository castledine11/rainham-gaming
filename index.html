Competitions

Designed for casual and competitive players

Keep the tone welcoming: short matches, clear rules, visible brackets and a friendly marshal for each competition area. This avoids confusion and keeps the event moving.

  • Use clear match rules before each round starts.
  • Display current brackets or waiting lists near each setup.
  • Have a simple tie-break process ready before the event begins.
  • Separate casual play from active tournament stations where possible.

No. The event is positioned as games, competitions and fun for everyone. Newer players should be able to join casual games and ask for help.

No. The advertised line-up includes console games and trading card games, including Pokémon TCG, Magic: The Gathering and Yu-Gi-Oh! TCG.

Entry is £2 per person.
